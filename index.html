Cass Burt

writer / author / designer

Biography

Born in Indiana, Cass got the hell out as soon as possible.
He first went abroad, teaching in Italy, the Czech Republic, and Poland before finding his way to sunny California.
After a short stint as a script writer and project manager for the Mighty Mighty Morphing Power Rangers guy (don't ask),
he finally landed his dream job when he was offered a position managing the University of Oregon's Midwestern Study Abroad Consortia.
He oversaw enrollment, communication, strategy, and even built their current website (a project he loved even if he totally cheated by using a CMS).

Then his academic unit (of which his program was a part) went bust.

Luckily, however, a new and interesting (if odd) offer came along - a chance to write a paid biography of a union boss who ran Illinois labor for 40 years.
Intrigued - and more importantly unemployed - Cass said yes.

And so, here we are.

Now in his third (and final) year of the book, Cass is beginning to worry about what comes next.
He is so worried that he has begun to write about himself in third person.
He is hoping this program might hold the key...
